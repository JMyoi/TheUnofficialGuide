About US
The Computer Science Department offers programs that prepare students for careers as computer professionals and/or for continued study. The major provides a broad-based background in computer science and includes courses in computer software, computer systems, mathematics, network security, and computer hardware. We are committed to offering courses that stay current with changing technologies. Our BS in Computer Science degree is accredited by the Computing Accreditation Commission of ABET (http://www.abet.org).
